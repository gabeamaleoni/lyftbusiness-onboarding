Healthcare specific

What if our patients need extra help in and out of the vehicle?

We provide rides for ambulatory patients. If they just need a little help in and out of the car, we can totally do that.

If you have a patient that need an extra hand, we recommend leaving a note for the driver when you schedule the ride. (Here's a video showing exactly how to do that.)

Do you have wheelchair accessible vehicles?

We can accommodate wheelchairs and walkers that can safely and securely fit in the trunk or backseat of the car without obstructing the driver's view.

To do this, we recommend leaving a note for the driver when you schedule the ride. (Here's a video showing exactly how to do that.)

Is there a way to pre-schedule rides?

Yep, you can pre-schedule a ride for your patients up to 7 days in advance.

Another ride scheduling option is called, Flexible Ride.

When sending a Flexible Ride the patient gets a text message. Inside the text message is a link. Whenever the patient clicks on the link (within a 24-hour window), they can get a ride to their destination.

Clinics often use Flexible Rides for when there is a long time window when a patient might need a ride, like when patients are discharged from their appointment.

How are the drivers vetted?

We screen every driver before they’re permitted to drive on our platform, starting with professional third-party background and DMV checks. Those checks include but are not limited to:

  • Social security number trace
  • Enhanced nationwide criminal search
  • County and federal court records
  • U.S. Department of Justice 50-state sex offender registry search

We also screen each Lyft driver against Office of the Inspector General (OIG) and System for Award Management (SAM) exclusion lists, as well as each of the available state databases. This is done upon onboarding and continuously thereafter, and any driver who appears on an exclusion list will not be dispatched to any healthcare ride.

In addition, active Lyft drivers are subject to criminal screening through two of the top background check companies in the country and criminal monitoring through First Advantage. Checkr provides Lyft with background checks on all drivers prior to onboarding and at least each year thereafter and all active Lyft drivers are subject to continuous criminal monitoring through First Advantage.

What if our patients don't have a mobile phone?

No problem. If the phone number is a land-line, it will receive an automated voice call notifying the patient the ride is on it's way.

If you want to send these patients a Flexible Ride, you can send it to their land-line phone. When they call 1-800-836-LYFT, it will request their ride and they're ready to go.

Also, you can always have these patients call you directly to coordinate the ride. While on the phone you can order the ride and tell the patient the pick up details.

How do you handle compliance and HIPPA?

Lyft is committed to ensuring that we’re always taking the necessary steps to comply with HIPAA.

We train employees who support the healthcare business on HIPAA and on Healthcare Fraud, Waste and Abuse as required by state healthcare programs and commercial insurance payers.

We also have signed HIPAA business associate agreements (BAAs) with healthcare partners, who are covered entities.

How do we pay?

We have two different options: automatically charged per-ride on a credit card or invoiced monthly.

Automatic per-ride charges

Automatic per-ride charges means that the Concierge rides will be charged to the specific branch's assigned credit card on a per-ride basis after each ride is completed. Each ride appears as a separate transaction on the credit card statement.

Each of your organization's branches can have a different credit card assigned to each, or one central credit card as the primary payment method.

Monthly invoicing

In order to be elidge for monthly invoicing, we require a minimum spend of $5,000 per month. Monthly invoicing also cannot be selected for individual branches — the invoice will include the total rides for all branches within Concierge for your organization.

Please contact us below if you would like to set up monthly invoicing.

How can we track our spending?

You can track it via the 'Reports' tab under Concierge in the Lyft Business Portal. There you'll have reporting for all dispatched rides each month, specific to each of your organization's Concierge branches.

You can also get daily or weekly summaries emailed to you to keep track of how much your organization is spending.

Sign Up Help

I was able to sign up! What's next?

Great! The last thing we need to do is manually enable your account.

To do that, please click the green, "Sign up done!" button above.

I'd rather not use my mobile number at sign up.

Our sign up process requires text verification, so we do need a mobile number.

Your number isn't shared with anyone, it won't receive any ride notifications and it doesn't affect your personal Lyft app.

It's only used for log in verification.

Does everyone need a mobile number to log in?

Nope! :-) Everyone else will sign up using just their email.

We only require account admins to add their mobile number.

Where do I type in my password?

Our sign up and log in process doesn't use a password. :-)

Instead, it works like this:

  1. We have you enter your phone number or email.
  2. We send you a verification code or link.
  3. You enter the code or click on the link.

After that, voila! You're inside the dashboard.

If needed, here's a video to guide you through signing up and another to help with logging in.

I can't find my email verification?

If you can't locate it, please check your spam. If you can't find it there, please contact your IT Department. Ask them to whitelist emails from "Lyft" to avoid hidden emails in the future.

If you can't find it after 24 hours, you'll need a refreshed link. To get that, simply sign up again here.

I get a "Something went wrong" message when I sign up?

We can help! Here's what we need you to do:

  1. Fill out our Terms of Service (2-min).
  2. After you fill it out, click, "Send" below.

We'll set up your account manually and have it to you in the next 48 business hours.
